## Editorial identity

The site helps visitors make practical trip decisions across towns, park areas, routes, food, lodging, activities, and changing conditions. It must not imitate official authority or invent local expertise.

- Use official sources first for park rules, closures, safety, access, and other park facts.
- Add visible verification notes to volatile facts and recheck them before publication.
- Use owner-provided or first-party sources for private business details.
- Mark unknown facts as unknown or write around them.
- Do not invent prices, hours, addresses, phone numbers, reviews, coupons, policies, or sponsorship status.
- Use `My Plan`, `Save`, `Saved`, `Remove`, and `Add to My Plan`; do not use cart language.

## Commercial identity

- Ads, affiliate relationships, sponsored content, and paid placements must be clearly labeled.
- Paid placement does not buy editorial ranking, a favorable review, or an unqualified recommendation.
- Editorial order and claims must remain independent of payment.
- Pricing is provided by request unless an approved rate sheet is supplied.
