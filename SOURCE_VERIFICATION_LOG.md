## Business and commercial verification

- Prefer the business's official website, owner submission, official booking page, or direct written confirmation.
- Record the exact source URL, date checked, reviewer, and fields supported.
- Do not infer family suitability, accessibility, price range, booking availability, reviews, discounts, or sponsorship.
- Coupons and featured offers require terms, validity dates, and direct business confirmation before publication.
- Paid or affiliate status must be recorded separately from editorial verification.
